The Story Behind TARAZ

hello welcome to Taraz Integration Workshop a social enterprise located in the heart of Syunik in the town of Goris we started our journey in 2021 with one big dream to preserve Armenian craft heritage while supporting women in difficult life situations our artisans are strong talented women many of whom were forced to leave their homes in Artsakh here at Taraz they find not just work but stability support and new opportunities we train them in sewing embroidery digital marketing and accounting all of this is part of the Femmes en Marche project which we run together with the French Cultural Center in Goris and now let's show you what we create each item is unique we offer textile products handmade embroidery and custom made designs our products have already reached customers in France and now we want to connect with more people in Armenia and across Europe when you buy from us you're not just choosing a high quality beautiful product you're supporting a real mission and helping change the lives of women for the better Taraz is about craftsmanship care and the power of community thank you for being with us follow us on social media share our story and become part of this meaningful journey
